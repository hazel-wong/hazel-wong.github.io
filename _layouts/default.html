About

Hazel Wong is a senior at the University of Michigan, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Chemistry. After taking a course on Computer Game Design and Development, Hazel had developed a strong interest game design.

Since January 2020, Hazel has been working with Unity and C# to build a variety of different games. She joined a student-led gamedev team of five named MacroHard Studio, and together, they made Back 2 Back, which won first place in a 48-hour game jam, and Yolked, which won first place in the class showcase. MacroHard Studio decided to continue working on Yolked during the summer with the goal of publishing the game on Steam. Hazel also has experience with C++, Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript, React and SQL.

  • 6 months of experience using Unity
  • 6 months of experience with C#
  • 6 months of experience using Jira for project management
  • Won 1st place in the EECS 494 + EMU Student Virtual Games Showcase with Yolked
  • Won 1st place in the WolverineSoft 2020 Shammy Game Jam with Back 2 Back
  • Showcased Yolked at the Student Games Festival, hosted by the SGDA
  • 2 years of experience with C++

Projects

Yolked!
(Work in Progress)

Seven weeks into development by a team of five, Yolked! is a single-player, action-adventure that follows the excruciating journey of an egg as it uses its human arms to make its way across the kitchen. Yolked! won first place at the EECS 494 + EMU Student Virtual Games Showcase.

Back 2 Back

Completed in two days by a team of five, Back 2 Back is an action, shooter game, where the player must control two characters facing back to back in order to kill/avoid all incoming enemies. Back 2 Back won first place at the Wolverine Soft Game Jam at the University of Michigan.
